Killing Floor III



















Release Date: March 2025

Platforms: PC (Steam & Epic Games Store), Playstation 5 (PS5), Xbox Series X|S

Developer(s): Tripwire Interactive

Publisher(s): Tripwire Interactive

The year is 2091. The Megacorporation Horzine has unleashed an army of bioengineered monstrosities called Zeds. Now the rebel group known as Nightfall stands between these infernal creations and the future of humanity.

In Killing Floor 3 you'll team up with up to five people and wage war against waves of zeds, earn dosh, unlock skills, and build the ultimate arsenal.
