Screamer



















Release Date: 2026

Platforms: PC (Steam & Epic Games Store), Playstation 5 (PS5), Xbox Series X|S

Developer(s): Milestone S.r.l.

Publisher(s): Milestone S.r.l.

Check out the Screamer in this reveal teaser trailer for this upcoming reimagining of the 1995 racing game from developer Milestone.

Screamer returns in the form of a futuristic, story-driven racing game, featuring animated cutscenes created in collaboration with Japanese animation studio Polygon Pictures, and featuring the voice talent of Troy Baker.

Combining plot themes of human will, revenge, love, and greed with classic sci-fi imagery, Screamer immerses players in the clash between personal desires and external forces that seek to control them.

This journey unfolds through the eyes of a diverse cast of characters involved in a street racing tournament organized by a mysterious figure.
