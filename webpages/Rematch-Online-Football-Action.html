Rematch: Online Football Action



















Release Date: Summer 2025

Platforms: PC (Steam), Playstation 5 (PS5), Xbox Series X|S

Developer(s): Sloclap

Publisher(s): Sloclap, Kepler Interactive

Rematch brings a fresh take on football with immersive third-person action, skill-based gameplay, and intense online competition. Team up with your friends and tackle, dribble, aim, and shoot your way to victory in fast-paced 5v5 matches where every move matters.
